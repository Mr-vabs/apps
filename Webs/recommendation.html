Best Patterns & Designs

Shirts:

  • Plain: Works best for a clean and sharp look.
  • Checks: Great for a casual or semi-formal look (like the ones in your uploaded images).
  • Printed: Avoid overly bold prints; subtle patterns work better.

T-Shirts:

  • Solid Colors: Best option for a stylish look.
  • Minimal Prints: Small logos or simple graphics work well.

Pants & Jeans: Stick to solid colors, avoid patterns.

Example Outfit Combinations

  • Casual: Light Brown Checked Shirt + Black Jeans + White Sneakers
  • Smart Casual: Tan Plain Shirt + Beige Chinos + Brown Loafers
  • Formal: Navy Blue Shirt + Charcoal Gray Trousers + Black Leather Shoes
